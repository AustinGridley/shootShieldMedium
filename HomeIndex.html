Rules of the game
You are trying to shoot your opponent. You can only shoot when you have bullets. When you reload, you gain one bullet. When you shield and your opponent shoots, you are safe and they lose a bullet. If you shoot without possessing any bullets, you automatically shield. If you both shoot, you both lose a bullet. First adversary to shoot while the other reloads wins. If you get five bullets (or your opponent gets four bullets) both of you can super shot. A super shot will automatically win, regardless of shield status, unless both of you super shot, then you both will lose four and five bullets respectively.
